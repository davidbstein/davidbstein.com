about

I currently live in NYC where I tinker on side projects and do some short-term contract work. I unfortunatly don't have time to take on any additional contracts in 2017.

I most recently worked in NYC as a director of engineering at Appboy in 2016 and 2017.

I worked as a software engineer at Dropbox from 2011 - 2015. I focused mostly on making large-scale data analytics accessable to non-engineers. I've also spent time on security incident response, prototyping, internal tools, and sales and support automation.

From 2009-2011 I did research in distributed robotics at MIT CSAIL, where I designed and built heterogeneous robot swarms to prove feasability of automated distributed construction of large structures.
