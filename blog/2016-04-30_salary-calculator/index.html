Compute and Compare Effective Income

While job hunting, I wanted a way to compare my offers that could take the different cost of living and tax rates into account. Hacked this thing up. It has hardcoded brackets pulled from tax-brackets.org - it is not super accurate but should give a kind of rough idea of how salaries actually compare.

I assume the user is single and has no dependants or other investments. Things are a little messier when those assumptions go away.

This will save your session locally, but does not send any data to a server (in fact, davidbstein.com is a static stite)
